Need Quick Answers?

Here are a few of the top questions travelers ask us about Sikkim & North Bengal.

Do I need permits for North Sikkim or Nathula?
Yes. We arrange the mandatory Inner Line Permits in advance for spots like Tsomgo Lake, Nathula Pass, Gurudongmar and Yumthang so your trip stays smooth.
When is the best time to experience snow?
Snowfall is common from December to March around Tsomgo, Nathula, Yumthang and Gurudongmar. We suggest carrying winter layers and buffer days for weather.
How many days do I need for a complete tour?
Plan 7–8 days to cover Gangtok, Pelling, Darjeeling or North Sikkim. Short on time? We can craft a focused 4–5 day itinerary around your must‑see list.
What about vehicles and driver support?
Every package includes vetted drivers, realtime updates and 24×7 assistance. Choose from Innova, Scorpio, Xylo, Tempo Traveller and more.
